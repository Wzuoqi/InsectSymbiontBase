Metagenome

Our database currently contains 1338 metagenome samples, covering various insect species and their microbial communities across different geographical locations and sequencing platforms. The raw data were first quality-controlled using Fastp, followed by taxonomic composition analysis through two workflows: Kraken2 and MetaPhIAn. Visualization was performed using Krona. Additionally, the metagenomes were assembled into contigs using Megahit, and binning was conducted with the Metabat2 workflow. High-quality MAGs were extracted based on CheckM results.
